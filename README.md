**AI industry research · foundation models · I build what I study.**

I analyze AI as both a technological system and an economic transformation — what models can actually do, and where the resulting value lands across the US and China ecosystems.

**Current research — *Who Captures Value in the AI Era?***
A layer-by-layer framework on the global AI value chain (semiconductors → cloud → foundation models → agents → applications), organized around two theses: *Scarcity → Control → Ownership* and *Value Creation ≠ Value Capture ≠ Attractive Investment*. I'm operationalizing it as a research tracker with quarterly earnings predictions committed before each call and graded after. Research sample and deck available on request.
